Workshop Themes

Together we discuss and explore the future of GenAI

This workshop provides a forum to exchange experiences, discuss arising best practices, and collaboratively define procedures for integrating AI-based tools into research and interactive systems. An expected outcome of this workshop is developing a practical insights report, which will document diverse work practices and capture creative strategies for incorporating GenAI into HCI research and practice. We invite researchers to share their visions, hands-on experiences, and specific prompts that foster creative solutions and support reproducible applications within the community. Submissions should follow the two-column ACM format with a maximum of three pages, excluding references. Detailed submission guidelines and workshop information are available on our website1. The workshop will be in person, with all on-site presentations and discussions. Submissions will be selected based on their quality and relevance and should be made via Conftool. Accepted papers will be published in the GI Digital Library. At least one author of each accepted submission must attend the workshop, and all participants must register for both the workshop and at least one conference day.
